AI Practice & Leaderboard Sync

These run through Cloudflare Pages Functions, configured once by the site owner — not per visitor:

  • GROQ_API_KEY — powers AI Practice (functions/api/ai-question.js)
  • SHEET_URL — your Google Apps Script Web App, for the leaderboard (functions/api/leaderboard.js)

Set both in your Cloudflare Pages project → Settings → Environment variables, then redeploy.
